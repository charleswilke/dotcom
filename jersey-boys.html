Pre-Show Welcome Message

Season 100 starts in style

This welcome message greeted every audience throughout our entire run at the Omaha Community Playhouse. The last piece to the puzzle, completed right before the show opened. It became the perfect way to set the tone, some folks even cheering as the boys scamper off at the end. You can feel the characters' personalities shining through before the actors even take the stage.
